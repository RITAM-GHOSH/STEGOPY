How Encoding Works

Our steganography tool hides your message in the image by making tiny, imperceptible changes to the color values of pixels. The modified image will look identical to the original to the naked eye.

Tips:
  • Larger images can store more text
  • PNG images work best for steganography
  • The encoded image will be saved as a PNG
  • Keep your message reasonable in length
